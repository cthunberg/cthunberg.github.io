About Us

Thuned LLC provides expert information security consulting and virtual (fractional) CISO services to organizations seeking to strengthen their security posture without the overhead of a full‑time executive.

Our team brings deep technical and strategic expertise across industries, helping align security initiatives with business objectives, manage risk, and ensure compliance.

Our Services

Security Strategy & Roadmapping

Develop multi‑year security plans aligned with business goals, risk tolerance, and budget.

Risk Assessment & Management

Identify, evaluate, prioritize, and treat information security risks using industry frameworks.

Policy & Procedure Development

Create clear, practical security policies, standards, and operating procedures.

Regulatory & Compliance Guidance

Support for GDPR, HIPAA, PCI‑DSS, ISO 27001, NIST, SOC 2, CCPA, and other regulations.

Security Awareness & Training

Design role‑based training, phishing simulations, and security culture programs.

Incident Response Planning

Build, test, and refine IR plans; conduct tabletop exercises and breach simulations.

Vendor & Third‑Party Risk Management

Evaluate network, cloud, and application designs for gaps and best‑practice alignment.

Metrics & Reporting

Define KPIs, produce executive dashboards, and report to boards and leadership.

Advisory & Board Reporting
