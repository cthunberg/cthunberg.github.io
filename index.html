About Us

Thuned LLC provides on‑demand, executive‑level security leadership for organizations that need strategic guidance without the overhead of a full‑time chief information security officer. Our virtual CISO model delivers seasoned expertise to align security initiatives with business objectives, manage risk, and ensure compliance.

Ready to strengthen your security posture? Get in touch today.

Our Virtual CISO Services

  • Security Strategy & Roadmapping: Align security initiatives with business goals and create multi‑year plans.
  • Risk Assessment & Management: Identify, assess, prioritize, and treat information security risks.
  • Policy & Procedure Development: Craft comprehensive, practical security policies and procedures.
  • Regulatory & Compliance Guidance: Support for GDPR, HIPAA, PCI‑DSS, ISO 27001, NIST, SOC 2, CCPA, and more.
  • Security Awareness & Training: Design and deliver role‑based training programs and phishing simulations.
  • Incident Response Planning: Build, test, and refine IR plans; conduct tabletop exercises.
  • Vendor & Third‑Party Risk Management: Assess and monitor the security posture of suppliers and partners.
  • Security Architecture Review: Evaluate network, cloud, and application designs for gaps and best‑practice alignment.
  • Metrics & Reporting: Define KPIs, produce executive dashboards, and report to boards and leadership.
  • Advisory & Board Reporting: Translate technical risks into business impact for informed decision‑making.
