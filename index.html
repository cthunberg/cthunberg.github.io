About Us

Thuned LLC is a boutique information security consulting firm dedicated to serving Chief Information Security Officers (CISOs) and security leaders. We understand the unique challenges you face in today's threat landscape and provide tailored solutions that align with your business objectives.

Our team consists of seasoned security professionals with deep expertise in risk management, compliance, incident response, and security architecture. We partner with you to build resilient security programs that protect your organization's most critical assets.

Our Services

Risk Assessment & Management

Comprehensive risk assessments to identify, evaluate, and prioritize risks to your information assets. We help you develop risk treatment plans that align with your risk tolerance and business objectives.

Security Strategy & Program Development

Develop and mature your information security program with strategies that align with industry frameworks (NIST, ISO 27001, CIS Controls) and regulatory requirements.

Incident Response Planning & Testing

Prepare for the inevitable with comprehensive incident response plans, tabletop exercises, and red team/blue team exercises to test your defenses.

Compliance & Regulatory Support

Navigate complex regulatory landscapes (GDPR, CCPA, HIPAA, PCI DSS, etc.) with expert guidance on compliance requirements and implementation strategies.

Security Awareness & Training

Build a security-conscious culture through customized training programs that engage employees and reinforce security best practices.

Vendor & Third-Party Risk Management

Assess and manage risks associated with third-party vendors and supply chain relationships to ensure your extended enterprise remains secure.
